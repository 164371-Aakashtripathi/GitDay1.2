Recipe for Egg_curry

Ingredients:

  • Boiled eggs
  • Redchili
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Salt
  • tomato
  • cumin seeds
  • green chili
  • garlic
  • turmeric
  • coriander

steps needed to prepare:

  1. Take boiled eggs in a bowl.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, salt, cumin powder and coriander powder.
  3. Mix the spices thoroughly.
  4. Take a pan of oil, put the eggs in the pan and saute.
  5. Take a pan and add cumin seeds, green chilli and chopped onion
  6. Saute them tilll golden brown and then add ginger, garlic, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, red chilli powder and salt.
  7. Mix them well.
  8. Add water and let it simmer.
  9. Now add tomatoes, mix well and add more water. Mix them thoroughly.
  10. Grind the mixture to get the perfect mix of spicy gravy.
  11. Put the eggs into the gravy and mix.
  12. Now add coriander leaves and mix. Bring to simmer.
  13. Serve hot.
